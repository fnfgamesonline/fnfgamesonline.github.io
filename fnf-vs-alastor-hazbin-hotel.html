Brief introduction to the game FNF Vs. Alastor: Hazbin Hotel

FNF Vs. Alastor: Hazbin Hotel is a fan-made mod of Friday Night Funkin’, where you face off against Alastor (Radio Demon) – a prominent character from the animated series Hazbin Hotel.

- Only 1 song: “Insane” – extremely catchy and challenging.

- Images and effects are typical of Hazbin Hotel, beautiful and vivid animation.

- Focus entirely on rhythm and reflexes.

How to play FNF Vs. Alastor: Hazbin Hotel

  • Use the arrow keys (or WASD) to follow the rhythm of the song.
  • Each time you press the wrong rhythm, you will lose points and lose.
  • There are not many levels – only one match but it is very tense.

Tips to play better

Get familiar with the rhythm of the song: Listen first to recognize the difficult part.
Turn off ghost tap (if available): Practice higher accuracy.
Focus on rhythm over speed: Tap at the right time is more important than tapping quickly.
Track Alastor's movements: Can help you guess the rhythm.

Practice difficult parts: Try to remember specific rhythm patterns.
